Frequently Asked Questions

Does answering questions really help the cause?

Yes, for every question you answer correctly Ripple counts it as a vote towards donating 100% of the donations to an organization for that cause.

Where does the money come from?

Through donations and through the banners you see on our website.

Are there other ways to help these causes beyond this website?

Our website attaches links to volunteering opportunities under each cause. This provides more opportunities if you want a more hands-on experience.
