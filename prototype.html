Ripple

small choices start change

Water? Meet droplet.

With the acceleration of communications and the growing awareness of real-world problems, the hardest part of making a change is starting. Here, anyone and everyone is capable of initiating change that ripples outwards to bring our users and the causes they care about towards a brighter future.

How does Ripple work, you may ask? First, choose a cause that you care about. Then, pick a topic where you’d like your knowledge to grow. With every question that you answer correctly, the cause you care about receives a “vote” towards receiving 100% of the money donated to Ripple this month. The more you learn, and the better you get at answering questions correctly, the closer you come to receiving funding for the causes you care about.

Alright! If you have any questions, suggestions for more organizations we should include on our site, or topics you’d like to learn about, please contact us. Donations are always welcome, as well. Happy learning!

-The Ripple Team
